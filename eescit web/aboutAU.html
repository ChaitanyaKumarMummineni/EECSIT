About Alliance University
Alliance University (AU) is a private university in Bengaluru, established in 2010 under the Karnataka Government Act No.34. The university is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC), New Delhi. It has academic affiliation from the Institute of Engineering and Technology [IET], UK. The university holds accreditation from prestigious organisations, including International Accreditation Council for Business Education (IACBE), USA.
